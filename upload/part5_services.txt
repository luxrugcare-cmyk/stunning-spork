2602: Why Persian & Oriental Rugs Need Specialist Care
2603: A standard carpet cleaner sees your Persian rug as just another dirty floor covering. We see it very differently. These rugs are constructed using techniques and materials that demand specialist knowledge:
2604: Natural Dyes: Traditional Persian and Oriental rugs use vegetable and mineral-based dyes that can bleed or shift colour if exposed to the wrong cleaning agents or excessive moisture. Kathy conducts dye-stability testing on every rug before any cleaning begins.
2605: Hand-Knotted Construction: The pile is attached to a warp and weft foundation through thousands of individual knots. Aggressive cleaning methods, high-pressure extraction, or incorrect handling can loosen knots, cause pile distortion, or damage the structural foundation.
2606: Wool & Silk Fibres: Premium wool has a natural lanolin content that gives it lustre and resilience. Silk fibres are extraordinarily fine and delicate. 2607: Age & Fragility: Antique rugs may have weakened foundations, brittle fibres, or areas of wear that require a gentler approach. Kathy identifies these vulnerabilities during her personal assessment and adjusts the treatment plan accordingly.
