Q: Will my curtains really not shrink? A: Guaranteed. Our 100% no-shrinkage guarantee is the foundation of our business. In 15+ years and over 5,000 cleans, we have never had a shrinkage complaint. If it ever happened, we would replace your curtains at our cost.
Q: Do I need to take my curtains down? A: Not at all. That’s the entire point of our service. We clean curtains exactly where they hang — no removal, no re-hanging, no hassle for you.
Q: How long does a home curtain clean take? A: Most residential appointments are complete within 2–3 hours, depending on the number of windows and curtain types. You’ll have fresh curtains by lunchtime.
Q: What types of curtains can you clean? A: Almost everything — lined curtains, sheers, blackout drapes, Roman blinds, Austrian blinds, pelmets, and swags. Kathy assesses every fabric type on site and adjusts our process accordingly.
