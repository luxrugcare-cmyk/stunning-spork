    23→   423→422: A: Absolutely not. This is our most important guarantee.

    25→   425→424: Our process: - Uses specialist dry-cleaning solutions applied directly to the fabric - Never saturates or penetrates the backing/lining - Extracts soil and solution simultaneously — fabric stays structurally stable - Is suitable for silk, velvet, linen, and all delicate fabrics
